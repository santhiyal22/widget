üöÄ Quick Start Guide

1. Getting Started: Use the Home tab to get a quick overview of your product management activities.

2. Managing Features: Navigate to the Features tab to add new features or view existing ones.

3. Viewing Roadmaps: Check the Roadmaps tab to access your timeline and Kanban views.
